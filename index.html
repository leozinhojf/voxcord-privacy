Privacy Policy – VoxCord

VoxCord does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

The extension only accesses the user's microphone for the purpose of recording audio messages within the Discord interface. This audio is processed locally in the browser and is not transmitted to any external server.

No personal information, messages, or user activity is collected, stored, or shared.

The extension does not use any form of tracking, analytics, or third-party data collection.
